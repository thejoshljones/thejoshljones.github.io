About me

Hailing from the suburbs of St. Paul, I now get to design for the paper I grew up reading, the Star Tribune.

I received a Bachelor's degree from the University of Minnesota's School of Journalism. My emphasis was visual journalism, with a minor in art.

I like rock climbing and cats, but not climbing cats. I enjoy craft beer, and hot coffee (no cream or sugar, please.)
